WHAT DOES THIS CHART MEAN?

Mouse over each dot to see how much energy the building is using. The size of each dot is proportional to the amount of energy it uses, so the largest dot has the highest energy consumption.

WHAT DOES kWh MEAN?

Scientifically speaking, one kWh (kilowatt hour) is equal to the power necesary to use a thousand watts of energy for an hour. In the real world, think of a kilowatt hour as the amount of energy needed to run a lightbulb all day.
